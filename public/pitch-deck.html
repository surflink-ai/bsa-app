The "Judging Co-Pilot"

ISA rules are clear: AI cannot autonomously score a heat. Technology must be a "Judging Aid" or "Video Arbitration" tool. HeatSync is designed from the ground up to assist judges with objective data — never to replace their subjective judgment.
